Our Sweet Story

From our kitchen to your heart

Meet Shaira

Hello, I'm Shaira, the heart and soul behind Cakes Bites. My journey with baking began in my grandmother's kitchen, where I learned that the secret ingredient to every delicious dessert is love.

After years of experimenting and perfecting my craft, I decided to turn my passion into a profession. What started as a hobby baking for friends and family has blossomed into a thriving custom cake business that celebrates life's sweetest moments.

At Cakes Bites, we believe that cakes are more than just desserts - they're edible art that tells a story. Each creation is thoughtfully designed and meticulously handcrafted to bring your vision to life.

When I'm not in the kitchen, you can find me exploring local markets for the freshest ingredients, sketching new cake designs, or teaching baking workshops in our community.

With love,
Shaira
